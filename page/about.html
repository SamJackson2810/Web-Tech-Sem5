About Us

SJR VaxGuard is dedicated to providing a seamless vaccine management system. Our mission is to ensure that everyone has access to the necessary vaccines to protect their health and well-being.

Established in 2024, we have been at the forefront of the fight against COVID-19, ensuring efficient distribution and administration of vaccines. platform helps streamline the vaccination process from appointment scheduling to follow-up reminders, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience for all users.

Our system supports various vaccines, including Sinovac, AstraZeneca, and Sinopharm, among others, which are approved for use by health authorities worldwide. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of safety and efficiency in vaccine management.
